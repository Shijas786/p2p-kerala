‚õΩ Gas Fees
The small "delivery charge" for blockchain transactions
Every time you move crypto on the blockchain (deposit, withdraw, send), you need to pay a tiny gas fee. It's like a postage stamp ‚Äî very small, but you can't send without it.
Action Gas Needed
Deposit to Vault ETH on Base  or  BNB on BSC
Withdraw from Vault ETH on Base  or  BNB on BSC
Send to someone ETH on Base  or  BNB on BSC
üí°
How to Get Gas
For Base: Send a tiny amount of ETH ($0.01‚Äì$0.05) from an exchange to your wallet on Base network.

For BSC: Send a tiny amount of BNB from an exchange on BSC (BEP20), or buy BNB from a BNB sell ad right here on P2PFather!
‚ö†Ô∏è
No gas = can't move crypto. Your crypto is still safe, you just need gas to move it. Keep a small amount of ETH/BNB for gas.
