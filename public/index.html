Protocol Workflow

The Liquidity Lifecycle.

01 Initialize Node

Connect directly via the Telegram interface. Your non-custodial wallet is deployed instantly on the Base or BSC network, granting you total sovereignty over your private keys and assets.

02 Secure Escrow

Assets are locked within a decentralized smart contract. No central authority can access or freeze your liquidity during the trade execution phase.

03 Atomic Settlement

Upon mutual signal confirmation, the protocol triggers an atomic release. Instant settlement, sub-cent transaction fees, and zero counterparty risk.
