How it started

I kept getting blocked
after sending money.

Across India, P2P crypto trading is part of daily life. You find a seller on Telegram, agree on a rate, send them INR on UPI, they send you USDT or USDC. Simple. Fast. But the whole thing runs on blind trust in a stranger.

I sent ₹12,000 to a seller. He blocked me the second it arrived. No crypto. No refund. Nothing I could do.

It wasn't just me. It kept happening to people around me. Every week someone in a local group would post about getting scammed. The problem isn't that people are dishonest — most aren't. The problem is the system has no safety net. One bad actor ruins it for everyone.

So I built P2PFather. The fix is straightforward: before any money moves, the crypto gets locked inside a smart contract on Base or BSC. The seller can't touch it. The buyer can't take it either. The contract only releases it when both sides confirm.

You upload your payment proof, and the trade completes safely. The whole thing runs directly inside Telegram — no new app, no MetaMask, no seed phrases.

Sellers list their USDT or USDC — amount, INR rate, payment methods accepted. Buyers browse live offers in the mini app and pick the best rate.

02

Crypto gets locked on-chain

The moment a buyer accepts an offer, P2PFather locks the seller's crypto inside the P2P Escrow Vault on Base or BSC. The seller cannot withdraw it.

03

Buyer sends INR

The buyer pays via UPI, NEFT, IMPS, or bank transfer — whatever was agreed. Then uploads a screenshot of the payment confirmation.

04

Crypto released. Trade done.

Once the seller confirms the payment landed, the smart contract releases the crypto straight to the buyer's receiving wallet. 0.25% fee on BSC, free on Base.

FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

01Do I pay gas fees when creating an ad? +
No. Creating a buy or sell ad is completely off-chain and free. You only pay a gas fee when you actually deposit crypto into the escrow vault before a trade starts. That's a one-time on-chain transaction on Base or BSC — usually under $0.05.
02Which blockchains does P2PFather support? +
We support two chains — Base (Coinbase's L2, for USDC) and BSC (Binance Smart Chain, for USDT). Both are fast and cheap. You choose the chain when you set up your trade.
03What tokens can I trade? +
Currently USDT (BEP-20 on BSC) and USDC (ERC-20 on Base). These are the two most trusted stablecoins, traded against Indian Rupee (INR) via UPI, NEFT, IMPS, or bank transfer.
04How does the escrow work? Is my crypto safe? +
When a trade starts, the seller's crypto is locked inside our audited smart contract on-chain. Neither party can touch it until both sides confirm. The contract only releases crypto to the buyer after the seller verifies the INR payment landed. If there's a dispute, an admin can step in to resolve it.
05What are the fees? +
P2PFather charges 0.25% on BSC and 0% on Base — deducted from the released crypto. There are no hidden charges, no monthly subscriptions, no KYC fees.
06Do I need to install any app or create an account? +
No. Everything runs inside Telegram as a Mini App. Just open @P2pFatherBot, connect your wallet, and you're trading. No new app download, no sign-up form, no seed phrase entry.
07What happens if a seller doesn't release my crypto after I pay INR? +
You can raise a dispute directly in the Telegram trade chat. An admin will review the payment screenshot and transaction evidence. If your payment is verified, the smart contract can be triggered to release the crypto to you. Your funds are always secured on-chain — no one can just disappear with them.
